South Indian Destinations

Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh and Telangana

South India is gifted with everything a tourist looks for. It has got beautiful hill stations, backwaters, wild life sanctuaries, ancient historical monuments, waterfalls and more. Enjoy a houseboat cruise at the famous Alleppey backwaters in Kerala, walk through the historical ruins of Hampi in Karnataka, visit the ancient temples of Tamil Nadu and much more. South Indian food is popular all over the world. Try the delicious South Indian cuisine yourself as you travel along the various destinations in South India.
